# Pricing and Billing Guide

## SavannahCloud Hosting
- Basic Tier: $15/month.
  - 2 vCPUs
  - 4 GB RAM
  - 50 GB SSD storage
  - 1 TB bandwidth
  - Best for small websites and student projects
- Pro Tier: $45/month.
  - 4 vCPUs
  - 16 GB RAM
  - 200 GB NVMe storage
  - Unmetered bandwidth
  - Best for growing web apps and e-commerce
- Enterprise Tier: Custom pricing starting at $150/month.
  - Dedicated bare-metal servers
  - Custom load balancing
  - 24/7 dedicated account manager

## Agentic-Flow AI
- Developer License: $30/month per seat.
  - Basic models
  - Standard API rate limits: 100 requests/minute
  - Community support
- Business License: $99/month per seat.
  - Premium predictive models
  - Higher rate limits: 1000 requests/minute
  - Priority email support
  - Custom data connectors

## Baso Enterprise API
- Starter Pack: Free up to 10,000 messages per month.
- Growth Pack: $50/month for up to 500,000 messages.
  - Rich media support
  - Read receipts
- Scale Pack: $200/month for unlimited messaging.
  - Custom webhooks
  - Advanced analytics dashboards

## Billing Rules
- Subscriptions are billed monthly unless otherwise agreed.
- Enterprise customers may receive custom billing schedules.
- Customers should keep payment methods up to date to avoid interruptions.
- For invoice questions, contact billing@savannahtech.co.zw.

## Refunds and Cancellations
- Monthly subscriptions include a 14-day money-back guarantee.
- Refund requests must be emailed to billing@savannahtech.co.zw.
- Refund processing usually takes 3 to 5 business days.
- Cancellations should be submitted at least 24 hours before the next billing cycle.
