# Support and SLA Guide

## Support Channels
- Billing inquiries: billing@savannahtech.co.zw
- General support: support@savannahtech.co.zw
- Enterprise requests may be handled by a dedicated account manager.

## Operating Hours
- Monday to Friday, 08:00 CAT to 17:00 CAT.
- System monitoring runs 24/7.

## Expected Support Response
- Standard support requests should be acknowledged during business hours.
- Enterprise customers may receive priority handling based on contract terms.

## Escalation
- For unresolved issues, the support team should escalate internally.
- Severe incidents should be handled with higher priority.
- Customers should provide screenshots, error messages, timestamps, and affected account details.

## Service Expectations
- Customers should keep billing and contact details up to date.
- Planned maintenance should be communicated in advance where possible.
- If a service issue affects business operations, customers should contact support immediately.
