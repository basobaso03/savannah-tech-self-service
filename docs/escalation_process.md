# Escalation and Internal Handoff Process

## Escalation Triggers
- The customer issue is company-related but not covered in the knowledge base.
- The customer asks for pricing, contract terms, or custom integrations.
- The issue requires a human decision or sales follow-up.
- The issue involves security, billing disputes, or service outages.

## Handoff Steps
1. Capture the customer contact details.
2. Record the topic of interest or the unresolved issue.
3. Send the request to the marketing or support team.
4. Confirm to the customer that someone will contact them soon.

## Data to Capture
- Customer name if available
- Email address
- Phone number
- Product of interest
- Short description of the request

## Internal Priority
- Billing and account access issues should be prioritized.
- Enterprise leads should be handled as high priority.
- Security incidents should be escalated immediately.
