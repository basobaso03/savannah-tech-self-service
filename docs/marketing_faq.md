# Marketing FAQ and Lead Capture Guide

## When to Use This Flow
- Use this flow when the customer wants more information but the knowledge base does not have the answer.
- Use it when the customer asks how to start, which plan to choose, or requests a callback.

## What to Ask For
- Preferred email address
- Preferred phone number
- Optional company name
- Optional product of interest

## How to Respond
- Thank the customer for sharing the details.
- Confirm that the request has been sent to the marketing team.
- Tell the customer they will be contacted soon.

## Example Follow-Up
- "Please share your preferred contact details (email or phone number), and I will send your request to the marketing team so they can contact you soon."

## Notes
- Lead data should be stored securely and handled according to company policy.
- Enterprise customers may need direct sales follow-up.
