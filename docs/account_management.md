# Account Management Guide

## Account Basics
- Customers should keep their dashboard login secure.
- Email addresses should be current so billing and support can reach the right person.

## Upgrades and Downgrades
- Hosting plans can be upgraded from the server dashboard.
- If usage grows, customers should move to a larger plan before hitting limits.
- Enterprise plan changes may require account review.

## Cancellations
- Cancellations should be requested before the next billing cycle.
- Customers should export or back up any needed data before canceling.

## Contact Updates
- If you change your email or phone number, update your account profile.
- For urgent changes, contact support@savannahtech.co.zw.

## Lead Follow-Up
- If a customer wants more information before buying, the assistant should collect preferred contact details.
- Marketing team follow-up should be used when a question is company-related but not fully covered in the knowledge base.
